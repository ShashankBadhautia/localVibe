# LocalVibe – Artisan & Small Business Marketplace

LocalVibe is a React-based e-commerce marketplace designed to help local artisans, crafters, and small businesses showcase their products, manage inventory, and reach more customers online. The platform provides a smooth shopping experience for customers while giving sellers a simple dashboard to manage products and orders.

---

## Problem Statement

Local artisans and small businesses often struggle to reach a wider audience because they depend heavily on physical stores, local events, or social media pages. Many of them do not have access to a dedicated online marketplace where they can easily display products, manage inventory, and receive customer orders.

LocalVibe solves this problem by providing a user-friendly marketplace that connects customers with local sellers. Customers can browse handmade and local products, add items to cart or wishlist, place orders, track order status, and leave reviews. Sellers can manage their product listings, monitor inventory, and view customer orders through a dedicated dashboard.

---

## Features

### Customer Features
- Browse local artisan and small business products
- Search products by name or category
- Filter products by price, category, rating, and availability
- View detailed product information
- Add products to cart
- Add products to wishlist
- Place simulated orders without real payment integration
- Track order status
- Submit product reviews and ratings

### Seller Features
- Seller dashboard
- Add, edit, and delete products
- Manage product inventory and stock status
- View customer orders
- Update order status
- Monitor product performance

### General Features
- Responsive and modern user interface
- React Router-based navigation
- Reusable React components
- LocalStorage-based data persistence
- Clean product catalog layout
- User-friendly shopping experience
